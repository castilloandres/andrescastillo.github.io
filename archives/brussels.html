Bruselas

Places to visit

  • La Grand Place
  • Place st catherine: tons of restaurants and bars.
  • Monts des arts
  • Palais de justice where you can see Brussels skyline then go down the elevator y and walk through Marollen passing by Sablon and you should arrive to the Grand Place.
  • Visit Parvis du St Gilles: hip/alternative neighbourhood with restaurants and bars.
  • Maison dandoy: must eat waffle.

Food:

  • Prelude: my favorite place in Brussels. They open only in the afternoons and they serve healthy, organic food.
  • Les crustaces: belgian brasserie. Really good moules et frites for 25 euros. I recommend the one with white wine.
  • Les clans des belges : belgian brasserie.
  • La fin de siecle: belgian brasserie.
  • 9 voisins: Belgian brasserie.
  • Huggies: good hamburguer. They make their own beer.
  • Nonna: buen italiano 15-20 euros
  • Fries: fritland, frit flagey, maison antoine.
  • Les cercle des voyageurs: fancy bar and resto, good price.
  • Les super filles du tram: best hamburguer in Brussels.
  • La bottega della pizza: best pizza in Brussels.

Bars

  • Monk: awesome flemish bar to start the with some beers. They have a restaurant inside that only serves three types of pastas: bolognaise, vegetarian and special of the month. Really good ones!
  • Saint gery: awesome square with tons of bars (mappa mundo, la machine, mezzo para bailar, cafe des halles)
  • Le pharmacie: best gins, the environment is an experience you have animals and an old pharmacy theme from the 18th century a bit gothic in a way. A bit expensive but worth it.
  • Jalousy club: you need a code to get in that you have to tell the doorman. You can find this code on the internet.
  • Fuze: hard techno disco.
  • Cafe belga: awesome beers and cocktails. Dancing is involved if you want. Young people environment/vibe.
  • De haus: good music good gins and free peanuts.
  • Bonnefooi: groovy house music.
  • Delirium: 1200 types of beers.

Near by cities

  • Ghent: 35 minutes by train.
  • Antwerp: 1 hour by train.
  • Brugges: 1 hour by train
