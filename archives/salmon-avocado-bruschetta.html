Salmon avocado bruschetta.

Ingredients:
  • Salmon
  • Avocado
  • Bread (preferably baguette or bakery bread).
  • Cream cheese/goat cheese
  • Lemon
  • Eggs
  • Cilantro (optional)

Process:

  1. Mix the avocado with cream cheese, spices and lemon.
  2. You put the avocado mix on the bread and you put the Salmon on it
  3. Boil an egg for 6 minutes.
  4. Heat the bread and spread the avocado/cheese mix on top.
  5. Add the salmon.
  6. Add salt and pepper.
  7. I personally add cilantro on top to give it a splash of freshness.
