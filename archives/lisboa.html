Lisboa

Places to visit

  • Belém Tower
  • Monument of "los descubridores"
  • Monastery of the Saint Jerónimos
  • Pastéis de Belém.
  • MAAT museum (Art, Architecture and Technology)
  • Tram 28
  • Á Brasileira Café: pastéis de nata, coconut, coconut with lime and walnuts.
  • Parc Rooftop: nice rooftop with chill, romantic and dancefloor ambiences.
  • LX Factory: nice artsy warehouse with nice restaurants (all types) and shopping ateliers. I recommend Praça!
  • Mirador of Santa Lucía: nice place to sit with a view and beers.
  • Ramiro: authentic raw marisquería (place to eat fish).
  • Baixa Mar: nice seafood dishes.
  • Pink street: if you really want to go party.
  • Timeout market: market with all types of restaurants. I recommend ground burguer for really nice burguers. Order the double cheeseburguer please.
  • Sé de Lisboa Cathedral.
  • Plaça do comercio.
