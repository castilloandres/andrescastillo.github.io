Avocado tuNA.

Ingredients:
  • Avocado
  • Tuna
  • Goat cheese.
  • Salt pepper.
  • Lemon

Process:

  1. Mix the avocado with cream/goat cheese, spices and lemon.
  2. You put the avocado mix on the bread and you put the Salmon on it
  3. Add tuna and mix.
  4. Apply to any surface: bread, pita, crackers.
