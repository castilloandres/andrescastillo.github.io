WooRank professional experience

About:

WooRank is a tech startup that started in 2009 and focuses on building SEO tools to help websites improve their rankings in search engine results (in plain english: WooRank helps websites appear higher in Google search results).

Team Dynamics:

Self-driven team formed by 8-10 people were we had standups every day. You could pick a project to work on from a backlog list. There were no pointing fingers when fixing problems so if you picked up a ticket you could ask around but taking full ownership of the task.
